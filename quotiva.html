OVERVIEW

Quotiva is an iOS application designed to deliver supportive, uplifting quotes and reflections that users can turn to when they feel overwhelmed, anxious or emotionally drained. The goal is to offer a gentle, visually soft experience that feels like a small safe space inside the phone.

ROLE & TECHNOLOGIES

I worked as the iOS developer and designer, handling both the UI and the core logic of the app.

Swift iOS Mobile UI User Experience Emotional Design
FEATURES

Some of the core features of Quotiva include:

  • Daily inspirational quotes focused on healing and reflection.
  • Simple, calm interface with soft colors and typography.
  • Navigation designed to avoid overwhelm and keep the user grounded.
  • Foundations ready for future personalization and user settings.
